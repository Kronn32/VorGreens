About Us

History

The journey of VOrgreens starts in 1990 when Susilo Widodo first opened the restaurant in central Jakarta with the hopes of providing healthy sustainable food to the people of Jakarta, and now after 30 years VOrgreens has opened up to 25 branches all across Indonesia delivering quality food that you can enjoy while building a sustainable future for our planet.

Vision

The journey VOrgreens aims to continue to develop as a franchise that will eventually be able to grow internationally to provide the people all around the world with healthy and delicious food that is sustainable. Vorgreens will continue pushing to create a sustainable future.

Mission

Serving our customers with delicious foods made with sustainable, quality ingredients sourced from local licensed farmers and employing all staff from the local area who are trained to produce the best experience for our customers.

Providing quality food with the freshest of ingredients
